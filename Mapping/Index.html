ASEAN Energy Efficiency Governance Structures

Vietnam

  • Ministry of Construction (MOC)
  • Ministry of Industry and Trade (MOIT)
  • Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment (MONRE)
  • Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST)
  • Vietnam Green Building Council (VGBC)
  • Local Departments of Construction

Thailand

  • Department of Alternative Energy Development and Efficiency (DEDE)
  • Department of Public Works and Town and Country Planning (DPT)
  • Local Administration Organizations (LAOs)

Malaysia

  • Energy Division under Ministry of Economy
  • Suruhanjaya Tenaga Energy Commission
  • Ministry of Science, Technology and Innovation (MOSTI)
  • Ministry of Natural Resources and Environmental Sustainability (NRES)
  • Malaysian Green Technology and Climate Change Corporation (MGTC)
  • Joint Committee on Climate Change (JC3)
  • Public Works Department
  • Malaysia Green Building Council

Indonesia

  • EBTKE (Directorate General of New, Renewable Energy, and Energy Conservation)
  • Ministry of Public Works and Housing (MPWH)
  • Provincial Government
  • National Standard Organisations (BSN)
  • Green Building Council of Indonesia

Singapore

  • Building and Construction Authority (BCA)
  • National Environment Agency (NEA)
  • Energy Market Authority (EMA)
  • Housing Development Board (HDB)
  • Singapore Green Building Council (SGBC)
